Timeline

YEAR 2064 OVERVIEW

The Great Restructuring & The Birth of the Bounty Hunter Era

The year 2064 marks a definitive turning point in the post-WWIII era. Following the establishment of the URNC and the forced dissolution of major PMCs—most notably Griffin & Kryuger (G&K)—a massive power vacuum emerged across the contaminated zones.

In response, a new class rose to dominance: Bounty Hunters. Disillusioned former commanders, wandering Tactical Dolls, and civilian survivors flooded the wastelands, taking on dangerous commissions from high-risk deliveries to ELID extermination.

The Rise of B.R.I.E.F.

Amid this chaos, B.R.I.E.F. emerged as the de facto governing body for bounty hunter operations. Unlike old military hierarchies, B.R.I.E.F. controls the lifeline of the new economy through three core functions:

  • Commission Review – Verifying legitimacy and difficulty of contracts
  • Transaction Security – Providing escrow services to protect both clients and hunters
  • Logistics Distribution – Supplying ammunition, repair kits, medicine, and T-Doll parts to frontier zones

By the end of 2064, B.R.I.E.F. had become the silent regulator of the post-G&K world—essential for survival, yet a constant reminder of the bureaucratic order the Commander chose to walk away from.
