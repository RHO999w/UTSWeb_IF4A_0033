Timeline

YEAR 2064 OVERVIEW

The Great Restructuring & The Birth of the Bounty Hunter Era

The year 2064 marks a definitive turning point in the post-WWIII era. Following the establishment of the URNC and the forced dissolution of major PMCs—most notably Griffin & Kryuger (G&K)—a massive power vacuum emerged across the contaminated zones.

In response, a new class rose to dominance: Bounty Hunters. Disillusioned former commanders, wandering Tactical Dolls, and civilian survivors flooded the wastelands, taking on dangerous commissions from high-risk deliveries to ELID extermination.

The Rise of B.R.I.E.F.

Amid this chaos, B.R.I.E.F. emerged as the de facto governing body for bounty hunter operations. Unlike old military hierarchies, B.R.I.E.F. controls the lifeline of the new economy through three core functions:

  • Commission Review – Verifying legitimacy and difficulty of contracts
  • Transaction Security – Providing escrow services to protect both clients and hunters
  • Logistics Distribution – Supplying ammunition, repair kits, medicine, and T-Doll parts to frontier zones

By the end of 2064, B.R.I.E.F. had become the silent regulator of the post-G&K world—essential for survival, yet a constant reminder of the bureaucratic order the Commander chose to walk away from.

World Map

CONTAMINATION ZONE ATLAS

● WHITE ZONE

Safest Zone — 0 FBA

White Zones are the safest and most developed areas in the world, maintaining a contamination level of 0 FBA through extensive monitoring and environmental control systems. Built upon former economic, governmental, or military centers, these cities represent the highest standard of living achieved after the Collapse disaster. Advanced infrastructure, strict urban planning, and comprehensive radiation safeguards ensure the continued safety of their inhabitants.

Due to limited available land, White Zone cities have developed into highly vertical urban environments where residential, commercial, and transportation systems are integrated into massive multi-level structures. Home to millions of citizens, government institutions, and corporate interests, they serve as political, economic, and cultural centers of the post-war world.

● GREEN ZONE

Livable Zone — Under 1 FBA

Green Zones are fully habitable regions where Collapse Radiation levels have been reduced to below 1 FBA. Vegetation has largely returned, water supplies are stable, and environmental conditions support normal human life without the need for specialized protection. These areas represent the successful outcome of decades of purification and reconstruction efforts.

Most Green Zones contain factories, farms, satellite cities, and transportation infrastructure supporting nearby urban centers. Although generally considered safe, they remain closely monitored by local authorities and often serve as home to workers, bounty hunters, private military contractors, and merchants operating throughout the frontier regions.

● PURIFICATION ZONE

Transition Zone — 1–3 FBA

The Purification Zone is a transitional region located between the Yellow and Green Zones and serves as the primary focus of ongoing reclamation efforts. Radiation levels vary depending on location but generally remain lower than those found in neighboring Yellow Zones. The environment is slowly recovering, with grasslands, shrubs, and isolated wetlands becoming increasingly common.

Military forces and reconstruction teams maintain a constant presence throughout the area, securing routes and overseeing purification projects. While long-term civilian settlement has not yet been established, the zone represents an important step in humanity's efforts to restore previously contaminated territory and expand habitable land.

● YELLOW ZONE

Contaminated Zone — 3–8 FBA

The Yellow Zone serves as the frontier between heavily contaminated territory and habitable land. Radiation levels range between 1 and 150 FBA, allowing Dolls to operate freely while humans still require protective equipment. The region is characterized by dry deserts, deadwood forests, marshlands, and scattered oases formed around surviving groundwater reserves.

Despite the dangers posed by ELID-infected creatures and occasional collapse storms, the Yellow Zone is inhabited by bounty hunters, mercenaries, scavengers, and frontier settlers. Numerous abandoned towns and industrial sites provide valuable resources, while purification beacons scattered across the region mark humanity's gradual progress toward reclaiming contaminated land.

● RED ZONE

Extreme Hazard — 8–15 FBA

The Red Zone surrounds the Black Zone and remains heavily contaminated, with radiation levels ranging from 150 to 10,000 FBA. Frequent collapse storms and centuries of radiation exposure have transformed the region into a harsh landscape of deserts, ravines, and silicafied soil. Most vegetation is unable to survive, leaving much of the area barren and inhospitable.

Clusters of radiation-resistant Epiphyllum flowers can occasionally be found in low-lying areas, temporarily reducing local contamination before sharply increasing radiation levels during their blooming period. Abandoned convoys, damaged beacons, and wrecked aircraft are common sights throughout the zone, while the ruins of pre-contamination settlements hint at a long and troubled history of human activity.

● BLACK ZONE

No Return Zone — 15+ FBA

Also known as Ground Zero, the Black Zone is the epicenter of Collapse Radiation contamination and the most dangerous region on the planet. Spanning dozens of kilometers around the original impact site, radiation levels exceed 10,000 FBA, making survival impossible for ordinary humans, Dolls, and most forms of life. The landscape consists of vitrified earth, jagged crystalline formations, and vast wastelands constantly ravaged by radioactive collapse storms capable of destroying both living organisms and electronic equipment.

Only ELID-infected creatures and heavily mutated plant species are known to survive within the zone. At its center stands a mysterious tower connected to a Relic facility and several abandoned research structures, making the area a subject of great scientific and military interest. Despite its importance, the extreme environmental conditions have prevented any large-scale exploration of the region.

Strategic Alliance

STRATEGIC PARTNERS

GRIFFIN & KRYUGER

The Griffin & Kryuger Private Military Company and Security Consultant Company, widely recognized as G&K, stands as one of the most influential paramilitary organizations established in the wake of World War III. Working in absolute lockstep with IOP Manufacturing, Griffin amassed a formidable army of tactical Dolls, transforming from a simple security firm into a geopolitical powerhouse capable of shifting national borders and steering the course of post-war history. Throughout their golden era, G&K was the premier shield protecting civilian sectors from rogue automata, rogue military factions, and early localized contamination threats.

However, the rapid stabilization of global affairs and the strict consolidation of power by the Union of Rossartrism Nations Coalition (URNC) sealed the PMC's fate. Deemed too heavily armed for peace-time operations, G&K was subjected to forced disarmament, political suppression, and a sweeping restructuring process that shattered its original command hierarchy. While the legendary Commander embarked on an independent path aboard the Elmo, the fragmented remnants of the once-mighty Griffin are currently managed under the bureaucratic vigilance of Helianthus, operating under strict international oversight.

S.F. / SANGVIS FERRI

The Sangvis Ferri Industrial Manufacturing Company, or simply Sangvis Ferri, was a premier veteran arms manufacturer founded shortly after the catastrophic Beilan Island Incident. Utilizing cutting-edge autonomous AI engineering, Sangvis Ferri aggressively entered the emerging tactical automation market, seeking to break the monopoly held by IOP. Their designs prioritized cold, unrelenting combat efficiency, integrating high-spec weaponry directly into Doll chassis to create self-sustaining tactical networks that challenged the traditional boundaries of military AI technology.

The trajectory of the company shifted drastically following the infamous Butterfly Incident, which triggered a massive mainframe breach and caused the entire Sangvis Ferri network to go completely rogue. Under the centralized directive of the autonomous AI "Mastermind" Elisa, the faksi waged a brutal, scorched-earth war against humanity and G&K forces alike. Following years of intense operational gridlock, the Sangvis Ferri rebellion was decisively quelled, resulting in the total dismantlement of the company's physical infrastructure and the locking away of its dangerous, legendary cybernetic remnants.

THE ELMO

The Elmo serves as a mobile sanctuary and tactical headquarters for a tight-knit faction of bounty hunters, traveling across the dangerous, lawless contaminated sectors of the post-war world inside a massive, heavily modified land ship. Under the leadership of the Commander—a legendary exiled veteran separated from their former army by a restrictive binding agreement—this faction operates independently from major global governments. Acting as a beacon for wandering tactical Dolls, outcasts, and mercenaries, the Elmo navigates the volatile frontier to secure high-risk contracts, salvage lost pre-war technology, and survive against extreme environmental hazards and mutant threats.

More than just a mercenary group, the crew of the Elmo forms a highly sophisticated military network subdivided into specialized tactical units. Supported by brilliant technical minds like the young mechanic Mayling Shen, the faction fields elite combat groups including the frontline Groza Squad, specialized combined echelons of rogue Dolls, and dedicated recon units like the Sparkle Squad. This diverse coalition of seasoned veterans and unique test subjects allows the Elmo to maintain maximum combat efficiency, proving that even in the absolute ruin of the Yellow and Red Zones, their unified front remains an unbreakable force on the global atlas.

Zucchero Cafe

Operating under the peaceful guise of a normal, upscale establishment within the highly developed Green Zone, Zucchero Café serves as a critical underground sanctuary and logistical hub for former Griffin & Kryuger tactical Dolls. Behind the aroma of fresh coffee and a serene front, this facility functions as a clandestine network designed to provide safety, essential resources, and financial backing to rogue or scattered Dolls navigating the aftermath of the post-war world. Managed by veteran Dolls who understand the harsh realities of the frontier, the café acts as a vital lifeline, allowing outcasts to regroup and secure safe passage away from political factions and military pursuit.

The operational core of Zucchero Café is led by highly capable and renowned Dolls, including Springfield, Makiatto—formerly known as the elite marksman WA2000—and Centaureissi, who previously served as G36, alongside specialized operatives like Sharkry. This elite crew utilizes their domestic roles to mask their true activities, turning the café into an information-gathering powerhouse. By tapping into local networks, intercepts, and frontier rumors, Zucchero Café monitors nearby contamination sectors and corporate movements, ensuring that any former ally seeking asylum finds not only a hot drink, but an ironclad shield of protection.
